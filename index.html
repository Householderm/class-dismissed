Why Class Dismissed

Generic calculators weren't built for you.

Teacher retirement is different. You likely have a defined-benefit pension, not a 401(k). Your income in retirement depends on your years of service, final salary, and your state's TRS formula — not just a savings balance. Class Dismissed handles the math that other tools miss.

  • 🏫
    Pension-First Design Models your defined-benefit pension as the foundation of your retirement income, not an afterthought.
  • 📅
    Years of Service Calculator Tracks how additional years in the classroom change your retirement payout — because staying one more year can matter a lot.
  • 🔒
    Completely Private Your salary, pension, and retirement data never leave your device. No cloud, no account, no data collection.
  • 🆓
    Free, Forever Built with appreciation for public school teachers. No subscription, no premium tier, no catch.
